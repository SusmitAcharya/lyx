Light YearsX Team

Light YearsX was a small team created by a group of students for play but it ended up impacting all there lives deeply in a way one can never forget. A little fun with some school friends to forming deep bonds which they would carry for the rest of thier lives! No matter how far or what state ones in, they knew that they would always have each other as a support by thier sides.

Light YearsX Meaning

Light YearsX was named in the thought of a star light years away which appears in our sky as a tiny dot in a sort of X shape. Although it is so small, yet it gives us light, it lights up the night sky without the moon and it guves joy and happiness to people even when it is so so far away. Light YearsX team members had formed equally deep bonds which would stay strong no matter how much far they are from each other. Thus, it was named Light YearsX or Lyx in short and the motto being 'Affecting lives Light Years Away!'
